Weekly hybrid classes on Sunday and Tuesday evenings, daily morning meditation, private and corporate sessions by request, and a yoga retreat once a year. Join in person across West London or live-streamed online, wherever you are.

01, hybrid

Yang & Yin

Sundays · 5:00 to 6:30pm · 90 mins
all levelsvinyasayin

A complete practice that balances effort with ease. We begin with a dynamic Vinyasa flow to build heat, strength and flexibility, syncing breath with drishti and bandhas through a Yang transition, then sink into a restorative Yin practice to soothe the nervous system. You'll leave deeply centred, nourished and renewed.

Donation based. Drop-in, monthly subscription (4 classes) or gift package (5 classes).

Yogasana

03, online

Morning Meditations

Weekday mornings · 7:15am UK · 20 mins
all levelslive onlinestillness

Start your day with stillness. We are training the mind to find quiet before the world gets noisy, sitting still, becoming curious about what's present, watching thoughts and feelings move through. You have thoughts, but you are not your thoughts. A short, grounding way to begin again, each day.

"If you can control the rising of the mind into ripples, you will experience yoga." Patanjali
